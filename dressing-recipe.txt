Title:
Balsamic vinegar and soy sauce dressing

Description:
Easy and quick, Japanese-style salad dressing. Just mix up all the ingredients, and everyone definetly loves this!

Ingredients:
50ml Balsamic vinegar
50ml Soy sauce (Japanese soy sauce  ex.kikkoman)
25ml Extra virgin olive oil
1tsp Rapadura sugar
2 pinches Kelp meal

Method:
1. Put all ingredients into a bowl. Mix well.
2. Set aside for about 30 minutes.
3. Dress your favourite salad with it!
